7 in 7 - Prototype 07

WEEK 9 - TUE, OCT 29TH, 2019

Thinking from a similar perspective as the Prototype 4 and also inspired by the anthropomorphic lamp prototype we saw in the class, I made this prototype as an exploration and supplement.

Guiding Questions

  • What if our daily object were also susceptible to external interruption and easily distracted just like us?
  • Would this kind of interaction with physical object bring reflection to people thus reducing interruption from internal?

Concept

A curious and susceptible lamp that would be as focused and distracted as I am, even worse.
