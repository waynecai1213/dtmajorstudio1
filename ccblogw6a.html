Prompt 6 - Discover, Scale, Name, Narrate, Problematize

WEEK 6 - SUN, OCT 6TH, 2019

Discover: Slow interaction is a design strategy aiming to bring people reflection when they interact with objects. How to bring mental rest for busy people with slow interaction?

Scale: How could we cultivate patience and bring mental rest to people living at a fast pace?

Name: How could we cultivate patience and bring mental rest in workplace for college students?

Problematize: How to visualize impatience so that people could reflect on it? How to make people aware of the defect of fragmented attention brought by technology?
